The Team

HPRC is a student organization at WPI and a subcommittee of the AIAA. The team is lead by the Captain and the Officer Board, composed of ten dedicated members. It is organized into two divisions, Rocket and Payload, each ran by a division lead. Additionally, the team has two advisors, Professor Blandino, a faculty member in the WPI Aerospace Department, and Jason Nadeau, president of the Lake Winnipesaukee High Powered Rocketry club.
